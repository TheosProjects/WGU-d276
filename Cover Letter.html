Application for Conservator Fellowship

Dear Selection Committee,

I am writing to express my interest in the Conservator Fellowship. My work centers on the careful handling of fragile records, the organization of difficult collections, and the recovery of spatial information from materials that cannot afford rough treatment.

Over the years I have developed a disciplined approach to cataloging, stabilization, and review. I pay close attention to process, because small decisions can determine whether a document remains usable or is lost to wear. I also value collaboration and clear handoff notes, especially when several people depend on the same archive.

What draws me to this opportunity is the chance to contribute to work that protects community memory. I would welcome the opportunity to support your team with dependable field experience, careful records work, and a steady respect for collection integrity.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I would be glad to discuss how my background could support your program.

Sincerely,
Marin Thistledown
