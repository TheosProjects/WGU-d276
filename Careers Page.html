Cover Letter

From: Marin Thistledown — Arcane Cartographer

Application for Conservator Fellowship

Dear Selection Committee,

I write to express my interest in the Conservator Fellowship. Over the last decade I have specialized in recovering and stabilizing fragile cartographic materials from wetland contexts. My practice combines careful fieldwork, experimental dye chemistry, and the preservation of encoded features on vellum.

During recent projects I developed a low-moisture translocation method that reduced handling loss by over 40% and a sigil-safe cataloguing workflow that preserves sensitive markings. These methods complement a teaching practice in which I coach apprentices in slow-hand techniques and hazard-aware surveying.

I am particularly drawn to this fellowship because of its emphasis on community archives and the conservation of vernacular maps. I would welcome the chance to contribute my practical experience and to learn from your staff.

Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to the possibility of discussing how my experience might serve your collection.

Sincerely,
Marin Thistledown
